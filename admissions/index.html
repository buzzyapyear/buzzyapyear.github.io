How to Enroll

First, attend a Parent Information Night where parents and the director will provide information about parent responsibilities, cost, and the developmentally appropriate curriculum.

Then, if you would like, you can schedule a visit to the school with your child where you will have an opportunity to observe your child in the school environment. You can either fill in the application form with a $40 non-refundable fee at the Information night or take the form with you and mail it in.

Our membership coordinator will contact you when a spot is available. If there is no current opening, then your child will be placed into the waiting pool.

Our next parent information nights will take place October 3rd and January 23rd at 7pm - 8:30pm. If you are interested in enrolling for the 2016-17 school year in the meantime, please contact membership@noevalleynurseryschool.com.

Monthly Tuition

Tuition is due at the first parent meeting of each month, and is based upon the number of days per week that the child attends:

  • For 5 days per week: $404
  • For 4 days per week: $327
  • For 3 days per week: $247

Scholarships are available by request. Requests for tuition reduction must be submitted in writing to the Board of Directors, and are given at their sole discretion.

Ages

Children must be between 2 years 9 months - 5 years old to attend Noe Valley Nursery School. Additionally, children must be potty trained upon enrollment. We do accept rolling enrollments, but the child must be age appropriate at the beginning of the school year.
